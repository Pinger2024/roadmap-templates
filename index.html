Creating Effective Roadmaps

Tips for building roadmaps that communicate clearly:

  • Focus on outcomes, not just deliverables
  • Show constraints and dependencies visually
  • Use consistent colour coding (RAG status)
  • Keep release buckets realistic and time-bound
  • Include "what's in each release" detail for stakeholders
